Our Story

Brew & Beans was born from a love of coffee and community. We roast our beans in-house, partner with local bakers, and foster a space where people can work, meet, and unwind over thoughtfully crafted drinks.

From sourcing to sipping, we obsess over flavor, sustainability, and connection—one cup at a time.
